About Z-Ray

AI-powered medical imaging analysis platform.

Our Mission

Z-Ray is built to assist medical professionals by providing fast, accurate, AI-driven analysis of X-Ray, MRI, and ECG data. Our goal is to reduce diagnostic turnaround time and enhance clinical decision-making with reliable, transparent results.
This Project is designed by team Zeta Minds.
